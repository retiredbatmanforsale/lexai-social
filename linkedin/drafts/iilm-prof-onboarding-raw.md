## The pedagogy (the proud part)
- We invented this pedagogy on Prof and are proud of it.
- It's an **engineering-first, open pedagogy**.
- It produces high-quality animation simulations using code — to make learning immersive, interactive, and gamified.

## The philosophy (the quotable core)
You don't understand a complex architecture by assuming it exists. You understand it by playing with its topology.
You don't understand calculus by assuming you know it computes the gradient. You play with different hyperparameters and the learning rate to see each effect come out in the picture.
You don't understand what GPT sees as tokens by assuming. You play around with tokenization.
You don't assume gradient descent works the same on convex and non-convex functions. You play around with that.

This pedagogy was made possible by engineering — engineering helped us solve this problem.
