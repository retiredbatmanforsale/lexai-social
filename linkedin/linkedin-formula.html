The Formula

Reach = ( Hook × First 90 min )CONSISTENCY

// a great hook earns the click · early engagement earns the reach · consistency makes it compound

01 Anatomy of a post

[ HOOK ]

Stop the scroll

Line one is the whole game. Only ~140 characters show before “…see more.” Open a gap or raise a stake — never warm up.

[ VALUE ]

One idea, well spaced

Deliver a single payoff. Short lines, real white space, no link in the body — native content gets distributed, exits get throttled.

[ ASK ]

End with a question

Close with something answerable in one breath. Comments outrank likes — and you reply to every one in the first hour.

02 Six laws

01

The first 90 minutes

Early comments + dwell time tell the algorithm to expand reach. Be present and reply fast right after you post.

02

Comments > reactions

Write posts people answer, not just tap. A reply is worth far more distribution than a like.

03

Links in the comments

Keep the post on-platform. Drop the URL in the first comment so reach isn’t penalised.

04

Show up 3–5× / week

Consistency compounds. The feed rewards regulars; sporadic bursts reset your momentum.

05

Headline = promise

Your headline is a value proposition, not a job title. State who you help and what changes.

06

Build in public

Narrate the real work — what broke, what you chose, what you learned. Founders who show the mess win.
